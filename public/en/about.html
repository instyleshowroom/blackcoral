HISTORY

California, 70’s: the "free spirit" became the unchallenged protagonist and femininity began to express itself through flowers, embroidery, lace and chiffon. In winter 2015, Black Coral made its debut, when its creators travelled to Los Angeles, with a single collection of trousers, named Alba: it was a one-size-fits-all garment that quickly became a real must-have. In jersey fabric, entirely printed and with a strong and eclectic style - the "flower power" inspiration is mixed with more contemporary references.

inspirations

Over the seasons, the collection expands and is enriched by garments that can meet the many needs in terms of look and wearing ease - but never forgets its origins.
Since Spring/Summer 2016, Black Coral actually has become a brand for those who want to express their style with personality. Year after year the image evolves, contemporaneity breaks out and the Fall/Winter 2019 collection marks a new beginning: Black Coral rethinks fit and patterns to update its language to that of the time.

crafts

Among the main refined fabrics used by Black Coral for its garments are silk, viscose, lurex and velvet. Soft lines keep playing a leading role, as do floral and multicoloured themes - yet the attitude of the customers is still free from rigid impositions.
Moreover, the exclusive prints are a constant source of inspiration and are often mixed using an artistic approach. All the garments are Made-in-Italy and no detail is left to chance: Black Coral does not renounce quality and the aesthetic vision of the brand always coexists with a perfect fit.
