Fall/Winter 19

Black Coral collections have bold prints, airy silhouettes and dress brave, independent, aware women.

This idea gave rise to one of the brand's symbols: a colour-changing, shaded cloud that recalls the Black Coral woman - always on the move, with some kind of lightness that is not only suitable for her clothes, but also for her personality. Black Coral is synonymous with a life discovered in its entirety.
